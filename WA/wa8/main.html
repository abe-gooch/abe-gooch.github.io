The Northern Colorado Caledonia Pipe Band (NCCPB) is a performance and competition band active in northern Colorado and the western United States. We work to recruit new membership and provide quality instruction in highland bagpiping and drumming for willing students of all skill levels. It is our goal to bring new members into the band regardless of their musical experience.



The Northern Colorado Caledonia Pipe Band is actively looking for new pipers and drummers and students. We welcome players of all ages and abilities! But you say that you don't know anything about Scottish pipe bands? That's fine too, we will instruct at all levels! If you are interested in joining or taking lessons, or are unsure, please contact someone in the band, or come to one of our practices!
